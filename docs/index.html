Unified Resolution Model (URM)

Author: Christo Cilliers
Release: July 2025

The Unified Resolution Model (URM) proposes that physical theories such as Loop Quantum Gravity (LQG) and M-theory are resolution-dependent views of a single underlying structure.

URM introduces a resolution parameter \( \rho \), a lens operator \( \hat{L}_\rho \), and a dimensional emergence function \( D(\rho) \), together forming a framework to simulate and interpret dimensional transitions in quantum gravity.
